U.S. Dermatology Partners

HTML Email Design + Development

Email marketing is a credible, measureable way to keep the business in touch with a targeted audience. I build email campaigns for U.S. Dermatology Partners to reach targeted audiences and increase brand awareness.

I created email blasts targeting U.S. Dermatology's current and former patients in an on-going campaign to increasing customer retention and engagement through personalization & education on skin care. Each email was written and designed to remind patients of the necessity of annual skin checks by a board certified dermatologist. Emails of these targeted campaigns have had an average open rate of 36%.

I also create monthly email campaigns announcing cosmetic specials which target the cosmetic clientele. Emails of these targeted campaigns have had an average open rate of 28%.

These emails were built using constant contact or solution reach – our patient engagement platform; each email implemented the use of HTML tables and inline CSS. Additionally, with 54% of all emails being opened on mobile - the emails are mobile optimized.
