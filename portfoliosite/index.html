Skills & Services

Out of the box designs that tell strong stories.

Graphic Design

Adobe Creative Suite
Illustrator & Photoshop
6 years

I first became a interested in design because I love art. I quickly realized that art and design are not the same and where art seeks to elicit emotion and connection between people; design communicates with the user to elicit action and solve problems.
The main tools I use in the creation of print collateral can be found in the Adobe Creative Suite; specifically Illustrator & Photoshop. I'm also proficient in InDesign & familiar with After Effects.
I design for both print and web media.

UI Design & UX Strategy

Sketch & Axure RP
1 year

Towards the end of my college career, I realized that User Interface Design combined my interest in design psychology/layout and communication.
I am goal oriented and always design with specific outcomes in mind. My standard process flow reflects this:

  • Ask A Myriad of Questions
  • Establish Site Goals & User Personas
  • Draft Flow Charts & Navigation Maps
  • Qualitative & Quantitative Data Analysis
  • Design Wireframes in Sketch
  • Prototype & Test Designs

Front-End Development

HTML5/CSS3 & Java Script
1 year

My development journey began in college, as I completed my undergraduate degree in Art & Technology; I took programing classes in Java & C++. I quickly realized these back-end languages weren't my cup of tea, however, the foundations of programing had been laid for my transition to front-end design and development for the web.
I proceeded to learn HTML5 & CSS3 and, more recently, I began learning Javascript to take my UI designs from conception to reality.
